## 🖤 Deep Tech Thoughts

### Thought #1 — What is “Nothing” in AI?

> In AI, “nothing” doesn’t exist.
> Even silence is stored as data.

### 💡 Breakdown

* No data → `null`
* No signal → noise
* No knowledge → random initialization
* Silence → zeros in tensors

### 🧠 Insight

In AI, absence is never truly empty.
It is always represented, encoded, and processed.

And maybe…

This isn’t just true for systems,
but for us as well.
