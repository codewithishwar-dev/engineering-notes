# AI May Write Code, But Humans Still Define the Problem

One day AI may write a significant portion of code.

But it will still depend on humans to answer one critical question:

**What problem are we actually solving?**

AI can generate:

- functions
- classes
- even entire applications

However, it still relies on humans to define:

- the real problem
- the constraints
- the trade-offs
- the system behavior

As coding becomes easier, **problem definition becomes more important**.

The future developer may not be the fastest coder.

It may be the person who understands the **problem space better than anyone else**.
